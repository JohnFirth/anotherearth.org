welcome to anotherearth.org

This application gives you a number of tools with which you can easily and comprehensively compare two views of the Earth side by side, using the Google Earth browser plugin. These include the ability to synchronize the movement of two Earths, select layers such as buildings, roads and borders for the Earths, jump to locations of your choice, undo/redo any movements, and save coordinates as a URL.

Please see the sub-site dedicated to the recent flooding in Pakistan.

Please refer to Google's documentation for guides to Google Earth and its navigation control.

If your browser's preferred language isn't English, and you have JavaScript enabled, then using a translator, powered by Google, I've attempted to convert the text.
